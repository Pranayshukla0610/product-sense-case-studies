# product-sense-case-studies
A curated collection of Product Sense case studies, frameworks, and structured problem-solving approaches designed for Product Management, Product Analyst, and Strategy interviews.

## Overview

This repository focuses on developing product thinking, user-centric problem solving, prioritization, growth strategies, and data-driven decision-making through real-world product case studies.

It includes structured approaches for tackling product interview questions commonly asked in Product Management and Product Analytics roles.

---

## Topics Covered

- Product Sense Frameworks
- Product Design Questions
- Product Improvement Case Studies
- Metrics & KPI Analysis
- User Segmentation
- Growth Strategy
- A/B Testing Cases
- Product Prioritization
- Root Cause Analysis
- Market Entry Strategy
- User Retention & Engagement
- Revenue & Monetization
- Product Analytics
- Go-To-Market Strategy
- Product Estimation Questions
